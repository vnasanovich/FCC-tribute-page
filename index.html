MAX FACTOR

The Father of make-up

Here's a time line of Max Factor's life:

  • 1872 - Born in Lodz, Russia (now part of Poland) on 5th August.
  • 1914 - Creates the first make-up for film, Flexible Greasepaint, to make actors appear more human.
  • 1918 - Introduces the Colour Harmony principle of make-up.
  • 1920 - Invents the word make-up and introduces Society Make-up to the general public.
  • 1928 - Creates Lip Pomade, later called lip gloss, to provide a glamorous, glossy appearance to movie actresses' lips on film.
  • 1932 - Builds a Beauty Calibrator to measure the dimensions of a person's facial structure to within 1/100th of an inch. This helps him determine how to use shading to create "the perfect face".
  • 1935 - Pan-Cake Make-up is developed to meet the unique make-up requirements of Technicolor film, as without corrective make-up to provide a natural complexion; actors' faces appeared brick red or blue.
  • 1938 - Dies at the age of 66.

"YOU ARE NOT BORN GLAMOROUS. GLAMOUR IS CREATED." - MAX FACTOR
